The Gourmet Bar

The Gourmet Bar is open to the public daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. The coffee is freshly roasted and responsibly sourced from Mexican producers and the cocktail offerings reflect our passion for agave-based cocktails. The wine list features a well-curated offering of local natural wines and European low-intervention boutique producers. Salud!
